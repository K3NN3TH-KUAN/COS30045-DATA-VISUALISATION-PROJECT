Mobile Phone Use Enforcement in Australia

How detection methods and enforcement changed from 2008–2024

@COS30045 Data Visualisation Project Team Member: Voong Zhe Hong, Alvin, Kenneth

Background

Mobile phone use while driving is a major road safety concern in Australia.

Before 2020, enforcement mainly relied on police officers observing drivers on the road.
From 2020 onwards, several states introduced AI-enabled mobile detection cameras, which significantly increased the number of fines issued — especially in New South Wales.
